QUEST TITAN

Quest has developed it’s own fully functional ERP system, geared towards the online survey industry. This state of the art system is available exclusively for Mindshare PM’s and a few non competitive clients. More availability within the industry is forth coming, but for now, it’s Mindshare exclusive. Titan brings the best of Quest together into one house. Client management, Project Management, Sample and Security Management and links together all trusted partners into one seamless and powerful tool. What does that mean for you? It means your project is not only run by the best team in the business, but is getting cutting edge management tech in Titan and cutting edge security in Quest Detect. Ensuring your data is safe, sound and reliable.

QUEST DETECT

The Online Survey industry is rife with Fraud. It is a continuous problem that effects everyone. Quest has invested significant capital addressing this issue by creating the Quest Detect tool. Leveraging cutting edge technology and working with a variety of non industry specialists, Quest Detect works within the scope of eCommerce and Financial services detection tools. Far exceeding industry standard tools. Everything from location detect, fraud signatures, device mining and intelligent response analysis, Quest Detect is the premier security tool in the sampling industry. Talk to your rep today about Quest Detect and field your surveys with peace of mind!
